Low-Carb, Low-Sugar, Low-Starch Meal Plan

Delicious, healthy meals to fuel your day!

Breakfast: Avocado and Eggs with Sautéed Spinach

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ avocado
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Chili flakes or paprika

Instructions:

1. Heat a skillet with olive oil or butter. Crack the eggs into the skillet and cook them to your liking (sunny-side-up or scrambled).

2. Add the spinach to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes until wilted.

3. Plate the eggs and spinach. Add avocado slices, and season with salt, pepper, and optional chili flakes or paprika.

Lunch: Grilled Chicken Salad with Olive Oil & Lemon Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • 2 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, or lettuce)
  • ½ cucumber, sliced
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • ¼ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Avocado or olives

Instructions:

1. Grill or bake the chicken breast until fully cooked.

2. In a bowl, combine mixed greens, cucumber, tomato, and onion.

3. Make the dressing by mixing olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour over the salad.

4. Slice the chicken and add on top. Optionally, add avocado or olives.

Dinner: Salmon with Cauliflower Rice and Broccoli

Ingredients:

  • 1 salmon fillet (5-6 oz)
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or coconut oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Fresh herbs (parsley, dill)

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Place the salmon on a baking sheet, season with garlic, salt, and pepper. Bake for 12-15 minutes.

2. Sauté cauliflower rice with oil in a pan until tender (5-7 minutes).

3. Steam or roast the broccoli until tender (4-5 minutes steaming or 10 minutes roasting).

4. Plate the salmon, cauliflower rice, and broccoli. Drizzle with lemon juice and garnish with herbs.

Dessert: Greek Yogurt with Chia Seeds and Berries

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup full-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp chia seeds
  • ¼ cup mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries)
  • Optional: A few drops of stevia

Instructions:

1. Stir chia seeds into the yogurt and let it sit for a few minutes to soften the seeds.

2. Top with berries and optionally sweeten with stevia.
